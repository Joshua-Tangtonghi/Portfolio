2D Character in 3D

Hello and welcome to my project presentation! In this project, I have taken on the challenge of modeling a 2D character in 3D using Blender. I taught myself how to use Blender and have become proficient in it over time. I started by studying the character design of my chosen 2D character and then translating it into a 3D model. I paid close attention to the details of the character's features and made sure to bring out their unique characteristics. The process of modeling the character was not without its challenges, but I persevered and worked through each obstacle. I made use of various tools and techniques in Blender to create the character's form and then added texture and color to bring it to life. As a result, I was able to successfully create a 3D model of the 2D character that captures their essence and personality. This project has been a great learning experience for me, and I am proud of the end result. Thank you for taking the time to listen to my presentation, and I hope you have enjoyed learning about my project!
